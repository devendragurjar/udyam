Frequently Asked Questions

Is Udyam really free?

Yes! Free trial for 30 days with all features. After trial, one-time license fee applies.

Does it work without internet?

Absolutely! Udyam works completely offline. Your data stays on your computer.

Can I use it on multiple computers?

Yes, with an Enterprise license. Standard license is for single computer use.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All data is stored locally on your computer, not in any cloud.

Do you offer training?

Yes! We provide video tutorials and can arrange one-on-one training sessions for Enterprise customers.
